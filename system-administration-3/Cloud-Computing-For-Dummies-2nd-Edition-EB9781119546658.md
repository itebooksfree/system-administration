Book Description
-----------------


    
    Get your head—and your business—into the Cloud&nbsp;
Cloud computing is no longer&nbsp;just&nbsp;a&nbsp;clever&nbsp;new toy in the world of IT&nbsp;infrastructure. Despite&nbsp;the&nbsp;nebulous name, it’s become a&nbsp;real&nbsp;and&nbsp;important&nbsp;part of our information architecture—and&nbsp;tech professionals&nbsp;who ignore it or try to skim their way through risk falling behind rapidly.&nbsp;The new edition of&nbsp;Cloud Computing For Dummies&nbsp;gets you up to speed fast, clarifying your Cloud options, showing you where can save you time and money, giving you ways to frame your decisions,&nbsp;and helping you avoid weeks of research.&nbsp;
In&nbsp;a friendly, easy-to-follow style,&nbsp;Cloud Computing For Dummies,&nbsp;2nd&nbsp;Edition&nbsp;demystifies&nbsp;the Cloud’s virtual&nbsp;landscape,&nbsp;breaking up&nbsp;a&nbsp;complex and multi-layered topic into simple explanations that will make the various benefits clear and&nbsp;ultimately&nbsp;guide you toward&nbsp;making&nbsp;the&nbsp;most appropriate&nbsp;choices for your organization.&nbsp;&nbsp;
Know the business case for the Cloud&nbsp;Understand hybrid&nbsp;and multi-cloud&nbsp;options&nbsp;Develop your Cloud strategy&nbsp;Get tips on best practices&nbsp;&nbsp;The Cloud&nbsp;is everywhere, and it can deliver amazing benefits to our lives and businesses. Get&nbsp;a&nbsp;much&nbsp;clearer&nbsp;vision of&nbsp;exactly&nbsp;how with&nbsp;Cloud Computing&nbsp;For&nbsp;Dummies—and&nbsp;you’ll&nbsp;begin to&nbsp;see that the sky really is the limit!&nbsp;&nbsp;
&nbsp;
