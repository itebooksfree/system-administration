Book Description
-----------------

An administrative professional&#8217;s job requires a diverse range of abilities. From managing schedules, coordinating meetings, and fielding calls to planning events, deciphering legal documents, and creating PowerPoint presentations, today&#8217;s administrative assistants are expected to effortlessly and efficiently juggle it all. Professionals looking to improve their performance&#8212;and enhance their value to employers&#8212;should turn to the fourth edition of Administrative Assistant&#8217;s and Secretary&#8217;s Handbook. With 250 pages of new or completely revised material, the handbook provides thorough, practical instruction on: Web conferencing &#8226; Creating graphics, charts, and presentations &#8226; Microsoft Office 2010 &#8226; Business math &#8226; Managing e-mail and schedules with Outlook &#8226; Organizing computer files &#8226; Research skills &#8226; Time management &#8226; Customer service skills &#8226; Troubleshooting computer problems &#8226; Event planning &#8226; and much more. Administrative jobs are constantly changing, but with the latest edition of this best-selling guide, professionals everywhere can stay ahead of the curve.
